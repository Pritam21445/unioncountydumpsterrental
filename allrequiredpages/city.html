Frequently Asked Questions in #State

Does my stuff actually get recycled?

Yes. We take our loads to certified local facilities where they are weighed and sorted. We pay more to take it there because we believe it’s the right thing to do. Materials like metal, concrete, cardboard, and clean wood are separated and sent to be processed.

Is it more expensive to rent from an eco-friendly company?

Not at all. Our dumpster rental prices are competitive with any other professional service in the area. In fact, if you can provide a "clean load" of a single recyclable material (like concrete or yard waste), we can often offer you a better price.

What is a "clean load"?

A clean load is a roll off dumpster that contains only one type of recyclable material. For example, a dumpster with 100% concrete, or one with 100% yard waste. These are the easiest and most valuable loads for recycling facilities.

Can I mix yard waste with a few old chairs I'm throwing out?

It’s much better if you don’t. Once you mix household junk or construction debris with your green waste, it contaminates the entire load, and it can no longer be composted. It all has to go to the landfill.
